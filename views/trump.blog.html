Visualizing Congressional Feedback

On Feb 28, 2017 President Trump addressed a joint session of Congress.

The President's speech touched on a number of themes - Immigration, Education, Healthcare along with others

And throughout the speech - Congress provided direct feedback through their applause


At MakeItMobile, we've done the hard work of annotating the entire speech. Every quote and the followup response from Congress - applause - has been documented

Each applause has been given a score based on its loundness and its duration

And by bucketing every applause and its score into a specific theme, i.e. Education, we can get an idea of the issues most important to Congress


Long story short - we've created an interactive graphic to explore the themes of President Trump's speech to Congress


Take this example:

At 29% of all feedback, Nationalism is the dominant issue of the night

Conversely, other themes - for example, Infrastructure, received only 1% of all feedback
